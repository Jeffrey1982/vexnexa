# VexNexa Brand Positioning Summary

## Core Position

**VexNexa is a fast, developer-friendly accessibility scanner that gives you real WCAG insights—without overlays, without sales calls, without the bloat.**

## One-Line Pitch

"Instant accessibility audits for developers, agencies, and SaaS teams who need real WCAG data, not widget band-aids."

## Target Audience

### Primary
- **Web developers** building accessible products from the start
- **Digital agencies** managing multiple client sites
- **SaaS product teams** shipping accessible features
- **Freelance developers** needing quick, credible audits

### Secondary
- Small marketing teams maintaining company websites
- E-commerce shops wanting to improve UX for all users
- Startup founders building inclusive products

## What We Are

✅ **A technical scanner** - Real WCAG/ADA analysis of your HTML, CSS, and ARIA
✅ **Instant and self-service** - Scan in seconds, no demo calls required
✅ **Developer-friendly** - Clear explanations, code-level insights
✅ **Transparent pricing** - See costs upfront, upgrade when ready
✅ **Multilingual platform** - English, Dutch, French interfaces
✅ **Overlay-free** - We scan your code, we don't inject scripts

## What We Are NOT

❌ **An enterprise compliance suite** - We're not Siteimprove or Level Access
❌ **An accessibility overlay** - We're not accessiBe or UserWay widgets
❌ **A legal guarantee service** - We provide data, not litigation protection
❌ **An automated fixer** - We show you what's wrong so you can fix it properly
❌ **A black box** - You get clear, technical WCAG findings

## Key Differentiators

### 1. No Overlay Controversy
We don't inject widgets. We analyze your actual code structure and give you real WCAG violations to fix at the source.

### 2. No Sales Friction
Enterprise tools make you sit through demos. We give you instant results and let you decide.

### 3. Developer-First
Built by developers, for developers. Clear technical language, not vague "compliance scores."

### 4. Transparent & Fast
See your accessibility score in seconds. Upgrade with a click. Cancel anytime.

### 5. Multilingual by Design
True international support with English, Dutch, and French interfaces.

## Competitive Positioning

| Competitor Type | Their Approach | VexNexa's Advantage |
|----------------|----------------|---------------------|
| **Enterprise platforms** (Siteimprove, Monsido) | Complex, expensive, requires sales calls | Instant access, transparent pricing, simple UX |
| **Overlay widgets** (accessiBe, UserWay) | Inject scripts, controversial in a11y community | Scan actual code, no widget injection, real WCAG data |
| **Generic auditors** | Basic checks, unclear reports | Developer-friendly explanations, actionable insights |

## Elevator Pitch (60 seconds)

"VexNexa is an accessibility scanner for developers and agencies. You paste in a URL, we scan it against WCAG standards, and give you a detailed breakdown of what needs fixing—organized by severity, with clear explanations.

Unlike accessibility overlays that inject widgets into your site, we analyze your actual code structure. And unlike enterprise platforms that require demos and contracts, we're self-service: scan instantly, see transparent pricing, upgrade when ready.

We're built for teams who want real data, not legal promises. Our reports tell you exactly what's wrong at the code level, so you can fix it properly. Available in English, Dutch, and French."

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid

❌ "VexNexa guarantees compliance" → ✅ "VexNexa helps you identify WCAG violations"
❌ "Protect yourself from lawsuits" → ✅ "Build more accessible experiences"
❌ "AI-powered automated fixes" → ✅ "Clear insights so you can fix things properly"
❌ "Trusted by Fortune 500" → ✅ "Built for developers who care about accessibility"
❌ "100% accessibility in minutes" → ✅ "Fast, accurate WCAG analysis"

## Visual Identity Notes

- **Modern, clean design** - Think Vercel, Linear, Stripe
- **Developer-friendly aesthetics** - Code snippets, clear data viz
- **Trust signals** - Technical accuracy, not fake testimonials
- **Fast-loading** - Practice what we preach on web performance
- **Accessible UI** - Our own site should score well

## Success Metrics We Focus On

- Time to first scan: < 10 seconds
- Free-to-paid conversion rate
- Developer satisfaction with report clarity
- Multilingual user adoption (EN/NL/FR split)
- Scan accuracy vs manual audits
