# VexNexa Pilot Outreach Playbook

Last updated: 2026-05-02

## Goal

Recruit 3 pilot customers who can validate the product with real scans, reports and remediation workflows before broader launch.

## Best-fit pilot profiles

1. Small or mid-size web agencies with 10-100 client websites.
2. EU-facing e-commerce or SaaS companies preparing for accessibility expectations.
3. Accessibility consultants who need branded evidence reports for clients.

## Pilot offer

- 30-day guided pilot.
- Scan up to 3 important pages or 1 small site crawl.
- Deliver a branded PDF report, issue workflow and remediation summary.
- Ask for one feedback call and permission to use anonymized product feedback.

## Qualification questions

1. How many public websites or client sites do you maintain?
2. Do you currently run accessibility scans before release?
3. Who fixes accessibility issues: developers, content team, or external partner?
4. Do you need client-ready reports or internal developer tickets?
5. Are you preparing for WCAG 2.2, EN 301 549 or EAA expectations?

## First outreach email

Hi {{first_name}},

I am building VexNexa, a WCAG accessibility scanning and reporting platform for agencies and teams that need clear evidence, not vague scores.

I am looking for 3 pilot partners. The pilot includes:

- a scan of key pages,
- a client-ready accessibility report,
- issue evidence with selectors and screenshots,
- a short remediation workflow review.

No long setup. I only ask for one feedback call so I can sharpen the product around real agency workflows.

Would it be useful if I scan one representative page and send you a short sample report?

Best,
Jeffrey

## Pilot success criteria

- The prospect can understand the report without a demo.
- A developer can reproduce at least 80% of findings using selectors and evidence.
- The prospect identifies at least 1 workflow or report improvement.
- The prospect is willing to be contacted again after fixes or monitoring.
